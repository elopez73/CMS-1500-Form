Loop 2310A
"Referring Provider Name"

Segments

"DN"
This segment will print out the Referring Provider's Last Name, if it's NOT empty. It has a maximum of 60 characters. (The Referring Provider is specified in the Account tab of the Patient Case).
This segment will print out the Referring Provider's First Name, if it's NOT empty. It has a maximum of 35 characters. (The Referring Provider is specified in the Account tab of the Patient Case).
This segment will print out the Referring Provider's Middle Name, if it's NOT empty. It has a maximum of 25 characters. (The Referring Provider is specified in the Account tab of the Patient Case).
